Stan Lee

Full name: Stanley Martin Leiber
Date of Birth: 28 December 1922
Last Day on Earth: 12 November 2018
Parents: Jack Leiber, Celia
Siblings: Larry Leiber(younger brother)
Stan Lee's Co-Created heroes: Daredevil, Hulk, Thor, IronMan, X-Men, Doctor Strange, SpiderMan and many more.

1922-1938

He was born on 28 December 1922 in Manhattan, New York, U.S.A. His actual name is "Stanley Martin Leiber". His parents were Celia and Jack Leiber. And he had a brother named Larry Leiber. Jack Leiber trained as a dress cutter and worked occasionally after the great depression. Stanley attended DeWitt Clinton High School in Bronx. You would be surprised to know how many part-time jobs he did in his youth.

  • part-time job as writing obituaries for news service and press releases for National Tuberculosis Center
  • Delivering sandwiches for Jack May pharmacy
  • Workes as office boy for a trouser manufacturer
  • selling subscriptions to NewYork Herald Tribune Newspaper
  • Ushering at the Rivoli Theater

At age 15, he participated in a competition held by New York Herald Tribune, and he won for straight 3 weeks. And the paper suggested that, to take up writing professionally.

1939

  • At age 16 and half, he graduated from high school.
  • He became assistant in Timely Comics(Later transforms into Marvel Comics). At that time writers/artists use ink pens to write/draw comics and they must have ink filled in their pots all the time. Stanley's job is to fill that ink-pots.
  • Stanley used to bring luch to the staff and used to do proofreading.
  • Stanley joined WPA Federal Theatre Project.

1941

  • Lee entered into writing comic book for the first time in timely comics. He had written a 2-page filler in the comic "Captain America Foils The Traitors Revenge"
  • This was his very first time he used his pseudoname Stan lee.
  • Then shortly after that, Stan Lee started writing actual full comic. That very first full comic is "Headline'Hunter,Foreign Correspondent". He co-created his first superhero character Destroyer, in Mystic Comics in August,1941.
  • Following this, he created Jack Frost(debut in USA Comics,August,1941), Father Time(debut in Captain America Comics,August,1941).
  • At this time, the creators of Timely comics, Jack Kirby and Simon left the Timely Comics. Stan Lee, a internim at that time, appointed as editor. As Lee showed a great potential, he continued as editor-in-cheif as well as art director for most of the time.

1942-1945

  • He joined US Army and served as a member of Signal Corps. He used to repair telegraph poles and other communication equipments.
  • During this World WarII time, he never neglected his comics work. He used to receive letters every week on friday from Timely Comics. He used to send his replies every monday.
  • He returned from army in 1945.

1945-1953

  • He used to live in a rented top floor of a brownstone in the East 90s in Manhattan.
  • Stan Lee is featured prominently as a story character in Margie #36 (June 1947).
  • Stan Lee got married to Joan Clayton Boocock on 5th of December,1947.
  • Stan Lee bought a house in Woodmere, NewYork, on long island and lived there through 1952.
  • In 1950, their daughter Joan Celia was born.
  • In 1953, lee and joan clayton had another daughter, but sadly she died within a few days after birth.

Late 1950's to Early 1960's

  • Stan Lee and Jack Kirby first created their own superhero team "Fantastic Four".
  • After the huge success of fantastic four, stan lee collabarated with so many artists and co-created many superheroes :
    Stan Lee and Jack Kirby together created Hulk, Thor, IronMan, X-Men.
    Stan Lee and Bill Everett together created Daredevil.
    Stan Lee and Steve Ditko created Doctor Strange, SpiderMan.

1965-1969

  • In 1965, Stan lee formed Merry Marvel Marching Society.
  • Following the success of Fantastic Four, in 1966, stan lee introduced a 3-Part Galactus Trilogy.
  • The Merry Marvel Marching Society became very popular by 1967. In 1967 Robbie Robertson created The Amazing SpiderMan.
  • In 1968, Stan Lee and Jon Buscema created Silver Surfer.
  • In 1969, Stan Lee and Gene Colan created Falcon, the first African-American superhero, in the Captain America comics.

1971-1977

  • Stan Lee indirectly helped reform Comics Code in 1971.
  • In 1972, Stan lee stopped writing monthly comics to assume the role of the publisher.
  • In 1977, Lee and John Romita Sr. launched the Spider-Man newspaper comic strip.

1990-2000

  • In late 1990's, Stan Lee stepped down from regular duties at Marvel.
  • In 1998, Stan Lee and Peter Paul founded StanLee Media which is an internet based superhero creation, production and marketing studio.
  • Near the end of 2000's , during an investigation , an illegal stock manipulation was discovered, which is done by Peter Paul and corporate officer Stephen Gordon.

2000-2010

  • In 2001, Stan Lee, Gill Champion, and Arthur Lieberman formed POW! (Purveyors of Wonder) Entertainment to develop film, television, and video game properties.
  • In 2002, Stan Lee filed a legal petition against Marvel claiming that company is not paying his shares properly.
  • Later in 2005, Stan Lee and Marvel settled their issue with a 7 figure amount.
  • From July 2006 to September 2007, Stan Lee created a reality telivision game show called "Who wants to be a superhero?". Stan Lee was it's host, judge and executive producer.
  • In 2008, Lee wrote humorous captions for the political fumetti book Stan Lee Presents Election Daze: What Are They Really Saying?
  • The Stan Lee Foundation was founded in 2010 to focus on literacy, education, and the arts.

Last Years(2012-2018)

  • In September 2012, Lee underwent an operation to insert a pacemaker.
  • On 6 July 2017, Stan Lee's wife Joan Clayton passed away due to complications from a stroke .
  • On January 10, 2018, Stan Lee was accused by multiple nurses of sexually harassing them at his home in early 2017.
  • At the age of 95, Stan Lee passed away, on November 12, 2018 due to cardiac arrest.
